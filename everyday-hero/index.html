Interested in making a difference at Home of the Innocents?

You can raise money to support our programs and services.

By creating a fundraiser, you play a leading role in providing help and inspiring hope to those we serve every day. With the donations you collect, you play a HUGE part in furthering our mission to enrich the lives of children and families with hope, health, and happiness!

Sharing your passion for the Home with your friends and family will create more awareness for the amazing work we do in our community. You are our biggest advocate and supporter and we can’t do what we do without you!

 

Click here to download our Everyday Hero Fundraiser Toolkit, with everything you need to get started!

FUNDRAISING IDEAS 

Select one of the options below to start your personal fundraiser for Home of the Innocents.

 

Celebrate your birthday for more than one day

Consider donating to Home of the Innocents in honor of your birthday by asking friends and family to give to your fundraiser instead of buying gifts this year.

 

Honor someone special

If you’d like to recognize someone important in your life, starting a fundraiser in their honor (or memory) is an amazing way to let everyone know how much they mean to you.

 

Share your happily ever after with the Home

Celebrate your wedding by having your guests make a donation to the Home in lieu of receiving wedding gifts.

 

Run. Walk. Ride.

Use your next athletic event to encourage people who love you to give to a cause you love. Get creative and set your fundraising goal to match how many miles you are biking or steps you are walking.

 

Have yourself a meaningful holiday party

Throw a holiday party and ask each of your guests to make a small donation to the Home. What better way to celebrate the season than by supporting a great organization!

 

Make giving part of your anniversary

Commemorate the start of something beautiful with a gesture of giving. Whether you are celebrating a wedding anniversary, the launch of a company, or a family reunion, there are countless ways to incorporate philanthropy into your festivities. Ask supporters to donate $1 for every year you are observing!

 

Graduate for good

Celebrate your college graduation or another educational achievement by starting a fundraiser. Pay it forward!

 

Set a personal challenge

Grow a beard, take a dip in ice water, or set a weight loss goal. It doesn’t matter what you do, as long as your friends would pay good money to see it happen. Make sure you post your progress on social media to get people pumped!
