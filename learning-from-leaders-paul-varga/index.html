Learning from Leaders: Paul Varga

Scott Drochelman, Director of Training

April 18, 2019

We’re excited to share a new podcast produced by Home of the Innocents called Learning from Leaders! In each episode, we point the spotlight at a different leader in the community for a behind the scenes look at what makes them unique.

 

 

Our first installment is with the former CEO of Brown–Forman, Paul Varga.

In this conversation, we talk about a wide variety of topics including:

  • Deciding on the right things to worry about.
  • How to be your genuine self even when feeling isolation and pressure.
  • The “why” behind your work.

Below is the audio to the episode, as well as links to two of Paul’s recommended books.

We hope you enjoy it! 

 

Books mentioned in this episode:

Grit: The Power of Passion and Perseverance

Good to Great: Why Some Companies Make the Leap and Others Don’t
